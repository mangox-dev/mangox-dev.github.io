Privacy Policy

Effective date: 19 May 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MangoX (“we”, “us”, or “the Service”) collects, uses, stores, and discloses information when you use the MangoX mobile application and related services. By using MangoX, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who we are

MangoX is a venue-based social application that lets adult users check in at participating venues, see other participants present in the same session, send hearts, view ranking interactions, and—when there is mutual interest—reveal contact between participants. MangoX is intended for users aged 18 or older.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Account & profile information

  • LINE Login identity. When you sign in with LINE, we receive your LINE user ID, display name, and (if you authorize it) profile image and email. We use this to create and authenticate your MangoX account.
  • Profile data you provide. Profile fields you fill in (such as nickname, age confirmation, basic introduction) are stored on our servers.
  • Profile photo. Photos you upload are stored on our servers and shown to other participants in your session under the conditions described below.

2.2 Photo & face quality validation

When you upload a profile photo, the image is processed by automated image analysis on our servers — including third-party services such as AWS Rekognition — to check basic quality, presence of a clearly visible face, and detection of inappropriate content. This processing is used solely to decide whether a photo meets MangoX quality and safety standards. We do not use this analysis to identify you outside the Service or to share facial data with third parties for advertising or biometric tracking.

2.3 Location data

To support venue check-in and geofence-based session matching, MangoX collects your device location while you are using the relevant features. Location is used to confirm that you are physically at or near a participating venue and to associate you with the correct session. We do not continuously track your background location for advertising purposes.

2.4 Service usage data

  • Hearts you send and receive (sender, recipient, timestamp, session).
  • Pick3 and ranking interactions in each session.
  • Reveal events when mutual interest occurs.
  • Check-in records, session participation, and venue presence.
  • Standard technical data such as device type, OS version, app version, IP address, and crash logs.

3. How we use your information

We use the information above to:

  • Operate the Service: authenticate you, place you into the correct venue session, match participants, deliver hearts and ranking interactions, and process reveals.
  • Maintain photo and profile quality so that the Service shows real, appropriate profiles to other adults in the session.
  • Detect and prevent fraud, impersonation, abuse, harassment, and unsafe behavior.
  • Monitor service performance, debug issues, and improve features through aggregated analytics.
  • Send you operational notifications related to your account or session.
  • Comply with applicable law.

4. How we share your information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following limited cases:

  • Other participants in your session. Your nickname, profile photo, and relevant interaction signals (hearts, ranking position, reveal status) are visible to other participants of the same MangoX session, consistent with the design of the Service.
  • Service providers. Infrastructure and processing providers (for example: Supabase for data storage, LINE for authentication, AWS Rekognition for image quality and safety checks, and our hosting/analytics providers) process data on our behalf under appropriate contractual safeguards.
  • Legal requests. We may disclose information when required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of MangoX, our users, or the public.

5. Data retention

We retain account and usage data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period afterwards as needed for fraud prevention, dispute resolution, safety, legal compliance, and service improvement. Profile photos and uploaded images are retained while your account is active and removed within a reasonable period after deletion.

6. Your choices & account deletion

You may at any time:

  • Stop using the Service.
  • Update or remove profile data via in-app controls where available.
  • Request deletion of your MangoX account and associated personal data.
Account / data deletion request.
Full in-app account deletion may be rolled out in a later release. In the meantime, you can request deletion of your MangoX account and personal data by emailing support@mangox.app from the email address associated with your account or by including your LINE display name and the approximate date of your last check-in. We will process the request within a reasonable period, subject to legal retention obligations.

7. Security

We use industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical safeguards (including transport encryption, server-side access controls, and least-privilege database policies) to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children

MangoX is for adults only. The Service is not directed to and may not be used by anyone under 18 years of age. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a person under 18, we will delete that information.

9. International transfers

MangoX may process and store data in countries outside your country of residence, including in regions where our infrastructure providers operate. By using the Service, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in those locations.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, notify users through the app or by other reasonable means.

11. Contact

If you have questions, requests, or complaints about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact us at support@mangox.app.
